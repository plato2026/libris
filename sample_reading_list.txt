## Ancient Philosophy

The Republic - Plato - 380 BC
Themes: Justice, ideal state, philosopher-kings

Nicomachean Ethics - Aristotle - 350 BC
Themes: Virtue ethics, eudaimonia, golden mean

Meditations - Marcus Aurelius - 170 AD
Themes: Stoicism, self-discipline, ethics

## Medieval Philosophy

Confessions - Augustine of Hippo - 397 AD
Themes: Autobiography, theology, philosophy of time

Summa Theologica - Thomas Aquinas - 1274
Themes: Natural law, theology, metaphysics

The Incoherence of the Philosophers - Al-Ghazali - 1095
Themes: Islamic philosophy, skepticism, causation
